Features

Everything you need to invest smarter

Built for long-term investors who want clarity, not complexity. Track ETFs, stocks, mutual funds, cryptocurrencies, and more.

Real-Time Prices

Investment prices are fetched from Eodhd (primary) or Yahoo Finance (fallback) with a smart cache layer. A source badge on every holding shows exactly where the price came from — real-time or cached.

Portfolio Analytics

Daily, weekly, monthly and YTD performance at a glance. See total gains, unrealised P&L, and how each holding contributes.

Allocation Chart

A live donut chart shows exactly how your capital is spread across your investments. Instantly spot if your portfolio has drifted.

Historical Evolution

Track how your portfolio has grown over time with a smooth area chart. Filter by week, month, year, or all time.

Future Projections

Model your wealth over 1–30 years using customisable return rates, monthly contributions, inflation, annual buy increases, and multiple Irish tax views.

Irish Tax Aware

Built-in Exit Tax, the 8-year Deemed Disposal rule, and SIA annual tax as an alternative yearly charge on the full portfolio value.

Portfolio Sharing

Share a read-only or collaborative view of your portfolio with a trusted friend, partner, or financial advisor in seconds.

Transaction History

Full purchase history for each investment — date, quantity, purchase price, total invested, live current price, gain/loss in EUR, and gain/loss % for every individual purchase. Green and red row highlights make profitable and underwater purchases instantly visible.

Projection Scenarios

Save multiple projection versions — bullish, bearish, base-case, or SIA-vs-Exit-Tax — and compare them side-by-side on a single chart.

Price Source Transparency

Each holding displays a source badge — Eodhd (premium), Yahoo Finance, or Cache — so you always know where the price data came from. Dashboard loads gracefully even when prices are temporarily unavailable.

Irish Tax Engine

No more spreadsheet nightmares for Irish investors

Irish investors holding ETFs, stocks, and other investments face some of the most complex tax rules in Europe — 38% Exit Tax, Deemed Disposal every 8 years, and no loss offsetting. Investments Tracker was built from the ground up with these rules baked in, while also supporting SIA annual tax as an alternative yearly charge on total portfolio value.

  • 38% Exit Tax on investment gains (not CGT)
  • Deemed Disposal modelled every 8 years
  • SIA annual % available as a replacement model for yearly portfolio-wide tax
  • Option to exclude pre-2026 holdings from Exit Tax
  • Year-by-year breakdown of every tax event, plus SIA rows in charts and tables
