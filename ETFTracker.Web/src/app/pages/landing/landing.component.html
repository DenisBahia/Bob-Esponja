Irish Tax Engine

No more spreadsheet nightmares for Irish investors

Irish investors holding ETFs, stocks, and other investments face some of the most complex tax rules in Europe — 38% Exit Tax, Deemed Disposal every 8 years, and no loss offsetting. Portify was built from the ground up with these rules baked in, while also supporting SIA annual tax as an alternative yearly charge on total portfolio value.

  • 38% Exit Tax on investment gains (not CGT)
  • Deemed Disposal modelled every 8 years
  • SIA annual % available as a replacement model for yearly portfolio-wide tax
  • Option to exclude pre-2026 holdings from Exit Tax
  • Tax Summary dashboard with pending vs paid totals and one-click mark-paid actions
  • Annual tax-free allowance configurable per investor type — applied year-by-year with a clear breakdown of allowance consumed vs tax remaining
  • Year-by-year breakdown of every tax event, plus SIA rows in charts and tables
For Every Investor

Powerful tools for investors everywhere.

Whether you're in the UK, Europe, the US or anywhere else — Portify handles CGT, inflation-adjusted returns and multi-asset tracking right out of the box.

CGT Calculations

Capital Gains Tax is calculated automatically. FIFO lot matching, allowable costs and net gain at your local CGT rate. Configure your annual tax-free allowance (e.g. the UK’s £3,000 exemption) and see it deducted per calendar year — so you always know your real tax position before you sell.

Inflation-Adjusted Returns

Your money’s purchasing power erodes over time. Every projection shows both nominal and inflation-adjusted “real” values so you know your true wealth trajectory, not just the headline number.

Multi-Asset, Any Exchange

Track ETFs, stocks, index funds, bonds and crypto. Prices fetched in real time from Eodhd and Yahoo Finance. Works for any exchange-listed ticker worldwide, with a smart source badge showing exactly where each price came from.

Scenario Planning
